OUR PORTFOLIO

First Free Medical Camp

On November 15, 2023, the first free medical camp was established at Government Elementary School in Shareen Muafi Village for the welfare of the general public. During the camp, over 160 patients received medical check-ups and consultations. This initiative aimed to provide essential healthcare services to the community, ensuring access to medical care for all.

Second Free Medical Camp

On December 17, 2023, the second free medical camp was held in Basipur, Okara. This initiative continued our commitment to community welfare by providing essential healthcare services to the local population. The camp successfully delivered medical check-ups and consultations, furthering our mission to ensure accessible healthcare for all.

Third Free Medical Camp

On March 10, 2024 our third medical camp was held again in Shareen Muafi. During this camp, around 200 patients received medical check-ups. To better serve the community, we organized two dedicated teams: Team A for male patients and Team B for female patients. This approach ensured that all attendees received appropriate and respectful care.

Fourth Free Medical Camp

On May 19, 2024Ishrat Fatima Hospital and Thalassemia Research Centre Pakistan established the 4th free medical camp at 7D Village near Churasta Mian Khan, Basirpur, Okara, Pakistan. This event marks a significant milestone in our ongoing efforts to provide essential healthcare services to underserved communities.
