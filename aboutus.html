ABOUT US

Welcome to Ishrat Fatima Medical Hospital a project of the Ishrat Fatima Trust, established on June 1, 2023, under the esteemed leadership of Chairman Main Ali Hassan Our hospital is dedicated to delivering exceptional healthcare services with a focus on compassion and patient-centered care. Over the past year, we have proudly conducted four medical camps, providing free medicines, checkups, medical treatments, and specialist referrals to those in need. At Ishrat Fatima Medical Hospital, we are committed to enhancing the health and well-being of our community through continuous innovation, excellence, and a deep sense of responsibility.



Assalam o Alaikum Friends! We all are aware of the sad state of health sector in our country, not everyone has the means to seek medical care from private hospitals as our private hospitals are not accessible to the poor Our government hospitals are not able to help patients suffering from catastrophic diseases. Our aim is to establish a private general hospital which will provide better healthcare facilities not only to people Irving in nearby areas but also to people living in remote areas.

Main Ali Hassan

Chairman, Ishrat Fatima Medical Hospital
